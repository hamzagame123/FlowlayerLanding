Project Overview

FlowLayer is a conceptual navigation application that introduces emotional routing ‚Äî a paradigm shift from optimizing purely for time or distance to optimizing for the quality of the journey itself.

In an era where autonomous vehicles and smart infrastructure are becoming reality, we asked: what if navigation understood not just where you want to go, but how you want to feel when you get there?

Key Learnings

üéØ

Simplicity Wins

Four vibes was the sweet spot. Earlier iterations with 6-8 options confused users. Clear, distinct choices lead to faster decisions.

üé®

Color is Communication

The vibrant color palette wasn't just aesthetic ‚Äî it became the primary language for communicating mood and route type.

üì±

Context is Everything

Users want different vibes at different times. A morning commute calls for "Chill" while a Friday night wants "Adventure."

üîÆ

Preview Reduces Anxiety

Showing what to expect on a route before starting dramatically reduced decision anxiety and increased route confidence.
