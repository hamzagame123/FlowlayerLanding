## Presentation Tips

### Timing
- **Total Duration**: 15-20 minutes for full presentation
- **Pitch Version**: 5 minutes (Slides 1-3, 9, 14, 24, 30)
- **Deep Dive**: 30+ minutes with Q&A

### Key Moments
1. **The Hook** (Slide 2) â€” Pause for impact
2. **Research Data** (Slide 6) â€” Use counter animations
3. **Four Vibes Reveal** (Slide 9) â€” Build anticipation
4. **Route Comparison** (Slide 14) â€” Interactive if possible
5. **Results** (Slide 24) â€” Celebrate the validation

### Demo Integration
- If presenting live, consider switching to the actual FlowLayer prototype
- Demo best placed after Slide 15 (Solution) or Slide 19 (Features)
- Show real-time vibe selection and route generation

### Audience Engagement
- Ask "Have you ever...?" questions during the Problem section
- Poll audience on which vibe they'd choose
- End with QR code to try the demo themselves
